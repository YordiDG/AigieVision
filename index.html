What is our Aegie Vision App?

Our app connects with your smart security cameras to protect your home or business. Receive real-time alerts for any suspicious movement and have peace of mind knowing that you’re protected at all times.

  • Automatically detect suspicious movements and receive instant alerts on your mobile device.
  • Integrate multiple cameras and adjust detection parameters for greater precision and customization.
  • Stay in full control from anywhere, reviewing footage and receiving notifications instantly.

1. Connect Your Smart Camera

Install and connect your smart security camera to start protecting your space.

2. Detect and Receive Alerts

Receive instant notifications whenever the camera detects unusual movements.

3. Review and Share

Access recordings, review captured events, and easily share evidence from the app.
